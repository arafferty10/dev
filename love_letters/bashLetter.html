Bash

Oh Bash,

How kind and fair. Oh bash how wonderful you are. For years I’ve wondered, tried, and experimented with you, never truly knowing your full potential (or even who you truly were). But now my eyes have been opened and there is no going back. You make me feel like a true coder, a real dork. Your black background and lime green text makes me feel like I live in the matrix.

What’s even better, is your newly available integration with Windows. I think you guys make a great couple, I’m not jealous at all I promise. Ubuantu seamlessly brings you two together and makes using you a breeze. Being able to run Ruby, Git, and Python with you directly on Windows is a dream come true. Can’t wait to see more of you.

Sincerely,

Aidan
