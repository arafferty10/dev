Github

Dear Github,

Thank you for making life so much easier. At first, I didn’t really know what to make of you, your purpose was obscure, your method seemingly complicated. But as we’ve spent more time together my love for you has grown. I can’t ever imagine working on a team project without you. Your ability to pull and merge files is unmatched. I don’t know how I ever collaborated on code before you came into my life. I look forward to many more repositories in our future.

Sincerely,

Aidan
